Safety & Suitability

Before You Begin

Your wellbeing is our first priority. All sessions begin with a professional consultation and suitability assessment.

Who Should Consult a Professional First

rightWELLNESS services may not be suitable for everyone. Please inform your wellness consultant if any of the following apply to you:

  • Fitted with a pacemaker or other implanted cardiac device
  • Currently pregnant or trying to conceive
  • Metal implants, pins, or plates in the treatment area
  • Active fever, infection, or acute inflammation
  • Skin disorders, open wounds, or active lesions
  • Undergoing active medical treatment or chemotherapy
  • Epilepsy or a history of seizure disorders
  • Thrombosis or known blood clotting conditions
  • Severe diabetes or circulatory disorders
  • Recent surgical procedures (within 6 weeks)
  • Under 18 years of age
  • Hypersensitivity to heat or electrical stimulation
Important Notice — rightWELLNESS services are wellness and aesthetic-care experiences, not medical treatments. We do not diagnose, treat, or claim to cure any medical condition. Results vary between individuals and are not guaranteed. Always consult your physician before beginning any new wellness programme, particularly if you have existing health conditions. Our trained wellness professionals will conduct a suitability review prior to your first session.
